You give the boy the bark and he accepts it without complaint. He pops it into his mouth and begins to steadfastly work his way through the earthy flesh.

"How many more days?" He asks, through bits of wood.

You do a quick mental calculation. The outbreak started three months ago, you and the boy have been on the road for two and a half...

You grimace. It'll take at least another two weeks.That's an optimistic evaluation. The rate the two of you are going, with all the stops and mishaps, it'll take you at least two before you reach Haven. But the boy doesn't need to know that.

Let him down. Offer optimism.
